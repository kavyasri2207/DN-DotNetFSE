Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a community event?

Return to the main portal and navigate to the "Register for an Event" section. Fill out your details, select your preferred event, and click "Complete Registration".

Why is my phone number showing an error?

Our system requires a valid 10-digit phone number. Please ensure you have entered all numbers correctly without any missing digits.

How do I find events near me?

Scroll to the "Find Local Events Near You" section on the main page and click the button. Make sure you allow location permissions in your browser when prompted!
